Developer by day Artist by night Brownie Eater 24/7

I am always looking for ways to translate my creativity in practical way.

As a result, I decided to take my interest in coding a step further, by leaving my job to train as a Junior Web Developer at General Assembly, and three months later, I haven’t looked back.

Coding gives me the ability to use my problem solving skills to create products in innovative ways. I love the idea of disrupting conventional ideas and finding new ways to improve on solutions for today’s problems and I want to be a part of this movement in ‘today’s’ digital world.

When I am not coding, I am mostly either indulging myself with anime or working on side projects for my art.

I am currently preparing myself for my first exhibition that is going to take place later this year.

My artwork is all about challenging societal norms and making the audience feel a certain way in order to spark conversations.
